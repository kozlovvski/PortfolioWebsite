Hi there!

My name is Michał Kozłowski and I am a freelance graphic designer who recently decided to move into front-end development. Currently I am looking for my first job in this field, so let me tell you a bit about myself :)

If you are more interested in my code, you can find my stack in My skills section, and my projects in Portfolio section.

I am 21 years old and I study at Warsaw School of Economics (SGH), where I am an active Students' Union member. During previous year I supervised marketing of an organization representing over 10.000 student enrolled at our University. My responsibility was to work with our graphic designers and make sure that our visual content is coherent and without any flaws. I conducted numerous workshops (in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop) thusly training over 200 members of our organization.

I got interested in front-end development while working in an internet marketing agency, where my job was to create landing pages in WordPress page builders (like Thrive Architect). Every now and then the tool we used didn't have a feature necessary for us and I had to code a custom solution to our problem - therefore I had to become friends with JS. I really enjoyed coding and decided, that I want to develop in this field.
